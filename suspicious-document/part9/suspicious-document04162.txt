Things Will Take a Turn.

By BEATRICE HARRADEN, author of "Ships that Pass in the Night." Illustrated. 12mo, $1,00.

The charm of this tale is its delicate, wistful sympathy. It is the story of a sunny-hearted
child, Rosebud, who assists her grandfather in his dusty, second-hand bookshop. One cannot
help being fascinated by the sweet little heroine, she is so engaging, so natural; and to love
Rosebud is to love all her friends and enter sympathetically into the good fortune she brought
them.
